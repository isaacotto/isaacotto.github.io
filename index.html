...Biography............................................


Isaac Otto is a composer, clarinetist, and saxophonist
currently pursuing his PhD in Integrated Composition,
Improvisation, and Technology at the University of
California Irvine in Southern California. His interests
include musical modernism, hybrid music notation,
algorithmic composition, cross-modal fixed/free musics,
the American jazz tradition and the music of the AACM.
He is also an avid draughtsman and film photographer.

Isaac holds an MFA from Mills College where he studied
composition/improvisation with Roscoe Mitchell, James
Fei, Fred Frith and Zeena Parkins.
