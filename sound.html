recent works

Live Forever N°˙ 1 --- a suite of improvised trio music recorded with no cuts and no overdubs at Specific Northwest in Los Angeles, California (January, 2024). For ease of listening it has been split into six tracks that, hopefully, demonstrate material unity and a meaningful arc. Isaac (alto sax ; clarinet ; bass clarinet) is joined here by Atticus Reynolds (drums) and the fabulous Dario Bizio (double bass). Listen on Bandcamp at the link above.

Claytosmunda Claytoniana --- for unaccompanied piano

"Interrupted" fern --- a very old species. All others in its genus are now extinct, as far as we know.

Modular No. XV --- for bass clarinet + effects, trombone, and trap kit

The fifteenth in a series of short pieces meant to be smashed up, broken apart, and put back together.

High Structure Carbon Black --- for solo horn --- play

"Disclosed herein are methods of synthesis and treatment, and dispersions and inkjet ink formulations prepared therefrom. The carbon black can have the following properties: OAN > 170 mL/100 g; and STSA ranging from 160 to 220 m²/g."

W/M --- for winds, 'cello, bass, and trap kit

A large, grimy warehouse holding detritus to be sorted for recycling.

XERODROMES I-IV --- For clarinets, kamancheh, tape machines, and little instruments

"By fulfilling creation's cycle of purification (dust to dust), the ground and biosphere contribute to the consummation of dust with wetness -- mess. The pro-creationist cycle of purification is transcendentally carried out through being recycled into dust over and over again." --- R.N.
